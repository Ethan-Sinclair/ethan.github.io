Accessibility

When creating a website it is important to think of the people who may be using your website. Ways to do this is to make sure you provide alternatives for users with auditory or visual impairments. For users with an auditory impairment, you should provide transcripts for any audio files. For users with a visual impairment there is many things you can do for example, make sure the colours used on the web page are suitable for users who are colourblind.

Why build a website?

Websites are a useful way of reaching an audience. They are a convenient way of spreading information worldwide and are used by most businesses. There is many different uses of websites ranging from shopping to informative to charity to gaming. Websites can be used for those purposes because of the use of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. A website can be used to advertise, sell, inform, entertain and for many other things.
